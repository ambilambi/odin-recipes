Berry Over Night Oats


Prep this fruity, creamy berry overnight oats recipe the night before, and you'll have a quick, fresh breakfast in the morning.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup quick or old fashioned oats, (such as Quaker Oats), uncooked
  • 1/2 cup nonfat milk
  • 1/2 cup nonfat plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds (Optional)
  • 1 cup fresh mixed berries and fruit

Directions

  1. Add oats to the container of choice; pour in milk.
  2. Layer in Greek yogurt, chia seeds, and mixed berries and fruit
  3. Refrigerate 8 hours to overnight
